12 Steps to Create the best PB and J!

  1. First You want to get your materials out and have them ready
  2. Then you want to make sure you have Bread, Jam, Peanut Butter and a spoon or butterknife.
  3. Then you may want to place your bread down on napkin.
  4. Then you want to open the peanut butter container.
  5. Then open the jam container.
  6. take the butter knife and grab some peanut butter with it.
  7. evenly spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  8. then sit the peace of bread down.
  9. then sit the butter knife down to be cleaned later.
  10. now grab the spoon and add a good amount of jam to the clean slice of bread.
  11. put the spoon down to be cleaned later.
  12. put both pieces of bread together and you have in your hand the best PB and J ever!
